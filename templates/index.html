HAPPY FATHER'S DAY!

I wanted to make this website in dedication to my father. If I were to say that I always got along with my father, I'd
unfortuately be lying(as would any other child my age). However, despite our differences, at the end of the day I know
how much my father cares about me and the rest of his family. He doesn't have to verbally say it, instead he's able to
show it by his actions and that, is ultimately much more meaningful than any long winded cheesy speech about familial
love. A father and son relationship is one bound by blood. It isn't something trivial that can be discarded when difficult
and upheld when convenient. It is permanent. My identity will forever be associated in some way with my father's,
and his with mine. Fortunately, in my case, I've been blessed to have a father whom I can be proud to be associated
with. I have the privilege of being the son of a morally upstanding, generous and overall great guy. To return
the favour, I now have the responsibility to be someone whom my father can be proud to be associated with.

Moving on, father's day is an opportuniy for children to express their respect for their fathers, but I've already
kind of done that. Repeating myself has no point and this website isn't going to write itself. Plus, as it's already been
done before I want to change things up. Therefore, I've decided to enlist some help. I've compiled the thoughts
of the rest of my father's family to show the universal respect all of his family members have for him.
